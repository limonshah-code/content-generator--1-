## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Best Side By Side Refrigerator

Choosing a major appliance like a side-by-side refrigerator is a significant investment. To ensure you make the right choice, consider these crucial factors before you buy.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
First, assess your household's lifestyle. Do you have a large family that buys groceries in bulk? A model with over 22 cu. ft. like the GE Profile might be necessary. Do you entertain often? A through-the-door ice and water dispenser, like the one on the Kenmore, is a must-have. For those in smaller apartments or condos, a counter-depth model with a smaller footprint, such as the Avanti or Iceblue, will be a better fit. Consider how you use your freezer versus your refrigerator space; the vertical split of a side-by-side offers easy access to both.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations ($X-$Y ranges)
Side-by-side refrigerators come in a wide price range. You can find excellent value models, like the Hamilton Beach, for approximately $900 - $1,300. Mid-range options with more features like advanced filters and dispensers, such as the Kenmore or Frigidaire, typically fall between $1,300 and $1,800. Premium and large-capacity models, including those from GE Profile or with built-in aesthetics like the Forno Salerno, can range from $1,800 to over $2,500. Set a realistic budget that includes potential costs for delivery, installation, and an extended warranty.

### Key Features - Most important technical specifications
Look beyond the basic cooling function. A through-the-door ice and water dispenser is a top convenience feature. Check for advanced cooling systems like multi-air-flow, which ensure even temperature distribution. Adjustable, spill-proof shelving adds flexibility and makes cleaning easier. Digital temperature controls offer more precision than manual dials. Also, consider energy efficiency—an ENERGY STAR certified model will save you money on utility bills over the life of the appliance.

### Quality & Durability - What to look for in construction
The longevity of your refrigerator depends on its build quality. Look for models with a stainless steel finish, as it's durable and easy to clean. A fingerprint-resistant coating is a huge plus for busy households. The heart of the refrigerator is its compressor; inverter compressors are generally quieter, more energy-efficient, and longer-lasting than traditional ones. Check for sturdy shelving and door bins that feel robust and not flimsy.

### Brand Reputation - Importance of established manufacturers
Sticking with well-established brands like GE, Kenmore, Frigidaire, and Hamilton Beach often provides peace of mind. These companies have a long history of producing reliable appliances and typically offer better customer service and more accessible replacement parts. Reading reviews for specific models can give you insight into their long-term performance and any common issues.

### Portability/Size - Considerations for space and storage
Measure, measure, measure! Before you fall in love with a model, measure the space in your kitchen where it will go. Remember to account for clearance on all sides—top, back, and sides—for proper ventilation. Note the difference between standard-depth models, which offer more capacity but stick out from countertops, and counter-depth models, which provide a sleeker, more integrated look. Also, measure your doorways, hallways, and stairwells to ensure you can get the appliance into your home.

## Is it A Wise Decision to Buy side by side refrigerator?

Investing in a side-by-side refrigerator is a very wise decision for many households, particularly for those who value organization and easy access to frozen foods. Unlike top-freezer or bottom-freezer models, the vertical, full-height freezer section allows you to organize items on shelves, making it much easier to see and retrieve what you need without digging through a deep bin. This can lead to less food waste and more efficient meal planning.

Compared to the popular French door style, side-by-side models often offer more dedicated freezer space and can be more budget-friendly. While they may have narrower refrigerator shelves, the convenience of having frequently used items in both the fridge and freezer at eye level is a significant long-term benefit. Furthermore, side-by-side units are the most common style to feature through-the-door ice and water dispensers, a convenience many families find indispensable. While the initial cost can be substantial, a quality side-by-side refrigerator is a durable, long-term appliance that enhances daily kitchen workflow, improves food organization, and adds value and functionality to your home.

## What Is the Best Way to Choose side by side refrigerator?

Choosing the best side-by-side refrigerator involves a systematic approach to ensure it meets your specific needs. Follow this step-by-step guide for a confident purchase.

**1. Accurately Measure Your Space:** Before anything else, measure the height, width, and depth of the available space. Don't forget to account for ventilation space (usually 1-2 inches on the back and sides) and measure the path it will take to get into your kitchen.

**2. Define Your Capacity Needs:** Consider your family size and shopping habits. A couple or small family might be fine with 15-20 cu. ft., while a larger family should look for models with 22-25+ cu. ft.

**3. Prioritize Key Features:** Make a list of must-have features. Is a through-the-door water and ice dispenser essential? Do you need adjustable shelves for tall items? Is a fingerprint-resistant finish important for your busy family? Ranking your priorities will help narrow down the options.

**4. Set Your Budget:** Determine a realistic price range. Remember that a more expensive, ENERGY STAR certified model might save you more money in the long run through lower utility bills.

**5. Research and Read Reviews:** Look at professional reviews (like this one!) and user reviews for the models that fit your criteria. User reviews can provide valuable insight into the long-term reliability and real-world performance of an appliance.

**6. Consider the Finish and Style:** Choose a finish that complements your kitchen. Stainless steel is timeless, while black stainless steel offers a modern edge. Ensure the handle style and overall design match your aesthetic.

**A common mistake to avoid** is focusing only on capacity (cubic feet) without considering the layout. A well-organized 20 cu. ft. model can feel more spacious than a poorly designed 22 cu. ft. one. If your current refrigerator is old but functional, it's often better to upgrade to a new, energy-efficient model rather than waiting for it to fail, as you'll start saving on energy costs immediately.

## FAQ

### What is the best side by side refrigerator for beginners?
For a first-time buyer, the Kenmore 36-inch Side-by-Side is an excellent choice. It offers a fantastic balance of essential features like a water and ice dispenser, energy efficiency, and a user-friendly layout. It's a reliable, all-around performer from a trusted brand that isn't overly complicated, making it a great entry point into modern refrigerators without an overwhelming number of niche features.

### How much should I spend on side by side refrigerator?
A good quality side-by-side refrigerator typically costs between $1,200 and $2,000. Models in this range offer a great mix of reliability, useful features, and energy efficiency. Budget options can be found for under $1,200, while premium models with advanced features or larger capacities can exceed $2,500. Your ideal budget depends on your capacity needs and desired features.

### What features are most important in side by side refrigerator?
The most important features are consistent cooling performance, a practical interior layout with adjustable shelves, and good energy efficiency (look for the ENERGY STAR label). For convenience, a through-the-door ice and water dispenser is highly sought after. A fingerprint-resistant finish is also a very practical feature for maintaining a clean appearance, especially in homes with children.

### How do I maintain my side by side refrigerator?
Regular maintenance is key. Clean the interior shelves and bins every few months with a mild soap solution. Vacuum the condenser coils on the back or bottom of the fridge twice a year to ensure efficient operation. If you have a water dispenser, change the filter as recommended by the manufacturer (usually every six months). Finally, check door seals regularly to ensure they are tight.

### Are expensive side by side refrigerator worth it?
Expensive models can be worth it if you value their specific features. These may include dual-evaporator cooling systems for better food preservation, smart home connectivity, craft ice makers, or a true built-in design. For most users, a mid-range model provides the best balance of performance and value, but if a premium feature will genuinely enhance your lifestyle, the extra cost can be justified.

### What brands make the best side by side refrigerator?
Several brands have a strong reputation for making reliable side-by-side refrigerators. GE, Kenmore, and Frigidaire are well-established names known for durability and a wide range of models. Brands like Hamilton Beach offer great value, while newer brands like Iceblue are making a mark with modern designs and competitive features. Choosing a reputable brand often ensures better customer support and parts availability.

### How long do side by side refrigerator typically last?
A well-maintained side-by-side refrigerator can be expected to last between 10 to 15 years. The lifespan can be influenced by the brand's quality, how it's used, and whether regular maintenance, like cleaning the coils, is performed. Models with more complex electronics or features might sometimes have a slightly shorter lifespan or require more repairs than simpler models.

### Can I find good side by side refrigerator on a budget?
Absolutely. Brands like Hamilton Beach offer excellent value, providing large capacities and modern features like digital controls at a very competitive price point. While you may forego some premium features like a water dispenser or the most advanced cooling tech, you can certainly find a reliable, spacious, and stylish side-by-side refrigerator without breaking the bank.
