## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Tabletop Jointers

Choosing the right tabletop jointer, whether it's a woodworking machine or connection hardware, requires careful consideration of your specific needs. This guide will help you navigate the key factors to ensure you make a wise investment.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
First, determine your primary goal. Are you a woodworker needing to flatten and square rough lumber? If so, a benchtop jointer machine like the OKYUK is what you need. Consider the width of the boards you typically work with—a 6-inch capacity is a great starting point for most hobbyists. If your goal is to connect existing tabletops, desks, or countertops, you need joining hardware. Ask yourself: Is the connection permanent or temporary?
