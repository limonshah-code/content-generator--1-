## What to Look for Before Buying the Best 20 Dollar Mini Fridge

### Find Your Need
Before you make a purchase, determine what you intend to store. If you only need to keep a few cans of soda cold or chill your morning skincare products, a 4-liter or 6-liter thermoelectric model is likely enough. However, if you are a college student or professional needing to store perishable lunch items, you should look for a 1.6 to 3.2 cubic foot unit with a compressor.

### Budget

Maintenance involves keeping the back vents clear of dust to ensure proper airflow. You should also wipe down the interior once a month with a mild soap and water solution to prevent odors. Yes, models in the $100+ range usually offer features like digital temperature control, compressor cooling, and significantly more storage space. These are worth it if you need to keep items at a very specific temperature or if you are using the fridge as your primary food storage in a dorm.
