## What to Look for Before Buying the Best 3 7 Qt Air Fryer

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations

### How do I maintain my 3 7 qt air fryer?
Always let the unit cool before cleaning. Use a soft sponge to preserve the non-stick coating and avoid metal utensils. Periodically wipe the heating element (when cool and unplugged) to prevent grease buildup, which can cause smoke during future use.
