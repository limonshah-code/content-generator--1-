## What to Look for Before Buying the Best 30 Inch Over The Range Microwave Black

Purchasing an over-the-range microwave is a long-term commitment. Unlike countertop models, these require installation and must match your existing cabinetry and range. Here are the most important factors to consider before you make your purchase.

### 1. Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
The first step is measuring your space. A standard 30-inch opening is common, but the height and depth of the unit can vary significantly. If you have low-hanging cabinets, a slim model like the COSMO Stellar is a better choice. You should also consider your cooking habits. If you rarely cook but reheat often, basic features are fine. If you use your microwave for complex recipes, look for sensor cooking and multi-stage programming.

### 2. Budget - Setting realistic price expectations ($250-$600 ranges)
Most quality 30 inch over the range microwave black units fall between $250 and $600.
- **$250-$350:** You will find reliable 1.6 to 1.7 cu. ft. models with 300 CFM fans and basic presets.
- **$350-$500:** These models often include larger capacities (1.9 cu. ft.), more powerful fans (400 CFM), and sensor technology.
- **$500+:** Premium models offer higher wattage (1500W), specialized finishes, and extended protection plans.

### 3. Key Features - Most important technical specifications
