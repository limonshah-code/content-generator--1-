## What to Look for Before Buying the Best 4 Cup Coffee Maker

Choosing the right small coffee maker involves more than just picking the one that looks best. To ensure you're happy with your purchase, consider these key factors.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
First, think about how you live and drink coffee. Are you a single person who needs one or two cups to start the day? Or are you brewing for two? A 4- or 5-cup model (which typically yields 20-25 ounces) is perfect for this. Consider your routine. If you're always in a rush, a model with a 'Pause and Serve' feature is essential.
