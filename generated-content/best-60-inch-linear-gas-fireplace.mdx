## What to Look for Before Buying the Best 60 Inch Linear Gas Fireplace

Choosing a 60-inch linear fireplace is a significant investment that impacts both the aesthetics and comfort of your home. Before you make a decision, it’s crucial to consider several key factors to ensure your new fireplace meets all your expectations.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
First, decide between gas and electric. A gas fireplace (Natural Gas or Propane) offers a powerful, realistic flame and significant heat output, making it ideal as a supplemental heat source. However, it requires professional installation, gas lines, and venting (for direct-vent models). An electric fireplace offers incredible installation flexibility, can be installed almost anywhere, and provides stunning visual effects with or without heat. They are perfect for ambiance and zone heating but won't typically heat as large an area as a gas unit. Consider your primary goal: is it powerful heating or versatile, year-round ambiance?

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations
Your budget will be a major determining factor.
- **Electric Models:** Generally more affordable, high-quality 60-inch electric fireplaces range from **$700 to $2,000**. The price variation depends on features like smart home integration, flame technology, and brand reputation.
- **Gas Models:** These are a more significant investment. Vent-free models can start around **$2,500**, while high-end direct-vent units like those from Empire can easily exceed **$5,000 to $7,000**, not including installation costs, which can add several thousand more.

### Key Features - Most important technical specifications
Look for features that enhance your experience. For electric models, this includes flame color options, ember bed lighting, smart controls (WiFi/Alexa), and included media (logs, crystals). For gas models, look at BTU output (higher means more heat), ignition type (intermittent pilots save energy), and control options (multi-function remotes are a plus). For both, consider safety features like a protective barrier screen and automatic shut-off.

### Quality & Durability - What to look for in construction
A fireplace should last for years. For gas units, look for high-grade materials like heavy-gauge steel and ceramic glass. Stainless steel is a must for outdoor models. For electric fireplaces, check for a sturdy frame, a quiet fan/heater, and quality LED lighting that will last for tens of thousands of hours. Reading user reviews can provide insight into the long-term reliability of a unit.

### Brand Reputation - Importance of established manufacturers
Stick with well-known brands that have a history of producing quality products and offering good customer support. Brands like Empire Comfort Systems, Dimplex, Touchstone, and PuraFlame have established reputations in the hearth industry. A strong warranty is also an indicator of a manufacturer's confidence in their product.

### Portability/Size - Considerations for space and storage
While a 60-inch fireplace isn't portable, its dimensions are critical. You need to measure your intended space carefully. For recessed installations, you must know the required framing dimensions, including width, height, and especially depth. Slim-profile electric models are now available that fit in standard 2x4 walls, while most gas units require much deeper framing. Always check the manufacturer's specifications and manual before beginning any construction.

## Is it A Wise Decision to Buy 60 inch linear gas fireplace?

Investing in a 60-inch linear fireplace, whether gas or electric, is a decision that pays dividends in both home value and quality of life. In an era where the home is a sanctuary, creating a central gathering spot that exudes warmth and sophistication is more valuable than ever. A linear fireplace instantly modernizes a space, serving as a dynamic piece of art that a static painting or piece of furniture simply cannot replicate. It elevates the ambiance of a room, making it cozier for quiet nights in and more impressive for entertaining guests.

Compared to traditional wood-burning fireplaces, linear models are far more convenient and clean. There’s no wood to chop, no soot to clean, and no chimney to maintain. With the press of a button, you have instant, controllable flames. While the initial cost can be significant, especially for high-end gas models, the value it adds to your home's resale value is often substantial. Realtors frequently cite a well-integrated, stylish fireplace as a key selling feature. Furthermore, the zone heating provided by these units can help reduce overall energy costs by allowing you to lower your central thermostat and heat only the room you are in. It's an investment in comfort, style, and long-term property value.

## What Is the Best Way to Choose 60 inch linear gas fireplace?

Choosing the right fireplace can feel overwhelming, but a systematic approach can lead you to the perfect choice. Follow these steps to make an informed decision.

1.  **Define Your Primary Goal:** Before looking at any models, decide what you want most from your fireplace. Is it powerful, realistic heating (leans toward gas)? Or is it modern aesthetics, color options, and easy installation (leans toward electric)? Your primary goal will narrow the field significantly.

2.  **Establish a Realistic All-In Budget:** Your budget must include not just the unit itself but also the cost of installation. For electric fireplaces, this might be a small cost for an electrician. For gas fireplaces, it will involve a certified gas technician and potentially a carpenter for framing and finishing, which can be a substantial expense.

3.  **Assess Your Installation Site:** Measure your wall space carefully. Determine if you have the required depth for a recessed unit. Can you run a gas line to the location? Is there an electrical outlet nearby? For direct-vent gas models, where will the vent terminate on the outside of your house? Answering these practical questions is crucial.

4.  **Compare Key Features:** Once you have a list of potential models, compare them feature by feature. Create a checklist: Smart controls? Included media? BTU output? Number of flame colors? Warranty period? Prioritize the features that matter most to you.

5.  **Read Reviews and Watch Videos:** Look for real-world user reviews and video demonstrations of the fireplaces you're considering. Videos are especially helpful for judging the realism and appearance of electric flames, which can be difficult to assess from static photos.

**Common Mistakes to Avoid:**
*   **Ignoring Installation Costs:** Don't be surprised by the total project cost. Get quotes from professionals early in the process.
*   **Choosing the Wrong Size:** A 60-inch fireplace is large. Make sure it is proportional to your room. A fireplace that is too large can overwhelm a small space.
*   **Prioritizing Price Over Quality:** A cheap, unreliable fireplace will be a source of frustration. It's better to invest in a quality unit from a reputable brand that will provide years of safe, enjoyable use.

## FAQ

### What is the best 60 inch linear gas fireplace for beginners?
For beginners, a smart electric fireplace like the **Touchstone Sideline 60** is often the best starting point. It's easier and cheaper to install than a gas unit, requires no venting, and offers simple controls via a remote, app, or even voice commands. It provides the beautiful linear look with minimal hassle and maintenance, making it a very user-friendly option.

### How much should I spend on 60 inch linear gas fireplace?
Expect to spend between $700 and $2,000 for a quality 60-inch electric model. For a gas fireplace, the cost is significantly higher, typically ranging from $3,000 to over $7,000 for the unit alone. Professional installation for a gas fireplace can add another $1,500 to $4,000 or more, depending on the complexity of the job.

### What features are most important in 60 inch linear gas fireplace?
For electric models, look for realistic flame technology, a variety of color and media options, and smart controls. For gas models, the most important features are BTU heat output, an energy-efficient ignition system (like an intermittent pilot), and a reliable multi-function remote control. For both types, a safety barrier screen and quality construction are essential.

### How do I maintain my 60 inch linear gas fireplace?
Electric fireplaces are virtually maintenance-free; just occasional dusting of the glass is needed. Gas fireplaces require annual servicing by a qualified technician. This service typically includes cleaning the glass, checking the gas pilot and burner, inspecting the venting, and ensuring all safety features are functioning correctly. This is crucial for safe and efficient operation.

### Are expensive 60 inch linear gas fireplace worth it?
Yes, often they are. Expensive models, like the **Empire Boulevard DV**, typically offer superior build quality, more realistic flames, higher heat output, and more advanced features. They are built with better materials and are designed to last longer. While a budget model can provide the look, a premium model delivers a more complete and durable experience.

### What brands make the best 60 inch linear gas fireplace?
For gas fireplaces, Empire Comfort Systems is a highly respected and established brand known for quality and performance. For electric fireplaces, brands like Dimplex, Touchstone, Modern Ember, and PuraFlame are leaders in the market, known for their innovative flame technology, smart features, and reliable designs.

### How long do 60 inch linear gas fireplace typically last?
A well-maintained gas fireplace from a quality brand can easily last 15 to 25 years or more. The longevity of electric fireplaces is typically determined by their LED lights and heating element. The LEDs can last for 50,000+ hours, while the heater and other components can last for 10-15 years, making them a durable choice as well.

### Can I find good 60 inch linear gas fireplace on a budget?
Finding a true gas fireplace on a tight budget is difficult due to the unit and installation costs. However, you can find excellent 60-inch electric fireplaces on a budget. Models from brands like Touchstone offer a wealth of features, including smart controls and multiple media options, at a very competitive price point, providing fantastic value.
