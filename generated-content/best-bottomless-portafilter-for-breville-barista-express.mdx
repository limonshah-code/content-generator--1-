Food-grade 304 stainless steel is the standard for the head. For handles, natural woods like walnut or rosewood are beautiful but require more care. Aluminum or high-quality ABS are great for those who want a tool that can be quickly wiped down without worry.

Bottomless portafilters are generally lighter and more compact than their spouted counterparts. If you have a low-profile machine setup, ensure the handle length is comfortable for your workspace and doesn't interfere with other equipment like grinders.

## Is it A Wise Decision to Buy bottomless portafilter for breville barista express?

Investing in a bottomless portafilter is a wise decision for any espresso enthusiast. It provides an immediate visual feedback loop that spouted portafilters simply cannot offer. You can see channeling, spurts, and uneven flow, which are indicators that your grind or tamping needs adjustment. 

Long-term, this tool helps you master the craft of espresso, leading to consistently better-tasting shots and less wasted coffee. While spouted portafilters are great for serving, the bottomless version is the ultimate training and diagnostic tool. It also allows for more crema and the use of larger baskets, which can expand your brewing capabilities beyond the stock limits.
