## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Craftsman Power Tool Combo Kits

Choosing the right combo kit is a significant decision. It sets the foundation for your workshop and locks you into a battery platform. Here’s what to consider to ensure you make a smart investment.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
First, assess the types of projects you'll be doing. Are you mostly assembling furniture and hanging pictures? A basic drill and impact driver kit might be all you need. Are you planning a deck build or a remodel? Look for a kit that includes a circular saw or a reciprocating saw. Don't pay for tools you'll never use. Also, consider the V20 battery platform. ### Key Features - Most important technical specifications
- **Brushed vs. Brushless Motors:** Brushed motors are the standard and are perfectly adequate for most tasks. Brushless motors are more efficient, run cooler, and provide more power and battery life. If your budget allows, a brushless kit is a significant upgrade.
- **Battery Capacity (Ah):** Battery capacity is measured in Amp-hours (Ah). A 1.5Ah or 2.0Ah battery is fine for light tasks, but for power-hungry tools like saws, a 4.0Ah battery or larger will provide much better performance and runtime.
- **Tool-Specific Features:** Look at the specs of the individual tools. For drills, check the max RPM and torque (measured in UWO or in-lbs). For saws, look at the blade size and cutting depth.

Choosing the right kit can be simplified by following a clear, step-by-step process. Avoid common mistakes and focus on what truly matters for your needs.

2.  **Set Your "Platform" Budget:** Instead of just a budget for the kit, think about the total system cost. A cheaper kit might seem like a good deal, but if the additional "bare tools" (tools without batteries) in that platform are expensive, it could cost you more in the long run. 3.  **Prioritize Brushless if Possible:** This is the single biggest technical decision. If your budget can stretch even slightly, opt for a brushless kit.
