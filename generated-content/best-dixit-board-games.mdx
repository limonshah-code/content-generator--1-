Choosing the right Dixit game or expansion can elevate your game nights from fun to unforgettable. Here are the key factors to consider to ensure you pick the perfect set for your group.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
First, determine if you are starting fresh or expanding your collection. If you're new to the game, you must start with a base game like the **Dixit (2021 Refresh)** or a standalone themed version like **Dixit Disney**. These include the scoreboard, voting tokens, and everything needed to play. If you already own a base game, you can explore the many expansions. Also, consider your audience. Are you playing with young children, a competitive group of adults, or Disney fans? Your choice of theme and art style should match your group's personality.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations ($X-$Y ranges)
Dixit games are generally affordable, offering great value for their replayability. A base game typically costs between $30 and $40. Expansions, which consist of a deck of 84 cards, are a more budget-friendly way to get a new experience, usually priced between $20 and $30. For a completely different but related experience, a standalone game from the Dixit Universe like **Stella** will be in a similar price range as the base game. Setting your budget helps you decide whether to invest in a new core set or simply refresh your current one with new cards.

### Key Features - Most important technical specifications
The most important feature of any Dixit product is the art. Each expansion has a unique artistic style, from the whimsical charm of **Memories** to the surrealism of **Daydreams**. Look at samples of the card art online to see which style resonates most with you. Another key feature is player count. The 2021 base game supports up to 8 players, while the standalone Odyssey game (not the expansion) can go up to 12. Most other versions and expansions are designed for 3-6 players.

### Quality & Durability - What to look for in construction
Asmodee and Libellud are known for their high production quality. The cards are typically made from a thick, durable card stock with a linen finish that makes them easy to shuffle and handle. The wooden meeples and other components in the base games are also well-crafted. Look for sturdy game boxes with good inserts, like the one in the 2021 Refresh, which helps protect your components and even store expansions. Proper care will ensure your game lasts for countless sessions.

### Brand Reputation - Importance of established manufacturers
Sticking with the official Dixit brand, published by Libellud and distributed by Asmodee, is essential. They are the creators of this unique game system and consistently deliver high-quality, beautifully produced games. Their reputation ensures that the artwork is top-notch, the rules are well-tested, and the components are durable. Off-brand or counterfeit versions will lack the quality and artistic integrity that make Dixit so special.

### Portability/Size - Considerations for space and storage
The standard Dixit box is a medium-sized square, typical for many modern board games. It's easy to store on a shelf but might be a bit bulky for travel. If you want a more portable option, you could take just an expansion deck and a simple voting method (like pen and paper) on the go. The thoughtful box insert in the newer base game is a huge plus for home storage, as it allows you to consolidate multiple expansions into a single box.

## What Is the Best Way to Choose dixit board games?

Choosing the right Dixit game is a personal journey into art and imagination. Following a few simple steps can help you find the perfect fit.

**1. Start with a Foundation:** If you are new to Dixit, your first purchase must be a base game. The **Dixit (2021 Refresh)** is the best starting point, as it contains all the necessary components (scoreboard, voting dials, player tokens) and a fantastic set of starter cards.

**2. Consider Your Gaming Group:** Think about who you will be playing with most often. If it's your family and you're all Disney lovers, the **Dixit Disney Edition** is a perfect, magical fit.
