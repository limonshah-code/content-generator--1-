2.  **Electric vs. Microwave:** Decide which type fits your routine better.

4.  **Read User Reviews:** Once you have a few candidates, see what real users are saying. Look for comments on durability, ease of cleaning, and consistency of cooking. This is where you can spot common issues or overlooked benefits.
