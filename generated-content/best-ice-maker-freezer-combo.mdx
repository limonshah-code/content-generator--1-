## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Ice Maker Freezer Combo

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations

**How do I maintain my ice maker freezer combo?**
Regularly use the self-cleaning function if your model has one. If not, clean the interior with a mixture of water and vinegar once a month. Ensure you change any water filters in full-size fridges every six months to keep the ice tasting fresh and to prevent mineral buildup in the lines.
