## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Irwin Tools Hand Drills

Choosing the right drill bit or extractor set can make a huge difference in the success of your projects. Here are the key factors to consider to ensure you select the perfect Irwin Tools set for your needs.

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations

**What's the difference between a screw extractor and a regular drill bit?**
A regular drill bit has right-hand flutes and is designed to cut and remove material to create a hole. A screw extractor has left-hand (reverse) flutes. It's designed to be used on a broken bolt or screw after a pilot hole has been drilled. As you turn the extractor counter-clockwise with a drill, its reverse threads bite into the sides of the pilot hole and unscrew the broken fastener.

**Are cobalt drill bits better than high-speed steel (HSS)?**
Cobalt bits are not necessarily 'better', but they are different and designed for a specific purpose. They are much harder and more heat-resistant than HSS, making them superior for drilling into hard materials like stainless steel. However, they are also more brittle. HSS bits are more flexible and are the better, more economical choice for general-purpose drilling in wood, plastic, and softer metals.
