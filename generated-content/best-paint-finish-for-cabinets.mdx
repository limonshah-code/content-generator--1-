## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Paint Finish For Cabinets

### 1. Find Your Need
Before purchasing, assess the material of your cabinets. While most all-in-one paints work on wood, laminate, and metal, some specialty surfaces like glazed tile or smooth plastics might require a dedicated primer or a specific formulation. Consider if you are doing a full kitchen renovation or a small touch-up, as this will dictate whether you need a gallon, a quart, or a small repair kit.

### 2. Budget
Prices for cabinet paint vary significantly. Small touch-up kits range from $15 to $25, while high-quality quarts typically cost between $35 and $60. If you are doing a large kitchen, a gallon refinisher can range from $90 to $150. Investing more upfront in a high-quality all-in-one paint often saves money by eliminating the need for separate primers and top coats.

### 3. Key Features
Look for self-leveling properties to avoid brush or roller marks. Low-VOC and water-based formulas are preferred for indoor use to avoid strong fumes. ## What Is the Best Way to Choose paint finish for cabinets?

To choose the best finish, start by identifying the lighting in your kitchen. Whites and light greys can look very different under warm versus cool LEDs. Use color cards or small sample pots to see the paint in your specific environment.

Next, decide on the sheen level. A velvet or satin finish is the standard for cabinets as it offers a nice balance between hiding imperfections and being easy to clean. Avoid extremely high gloss unless your surfaces are perfectly smooth, as gloss highlights every dent and grain.

Always follow a step-by-step selection guide:
1. Clean surfaces thoroughly with a degreaser.
2. Test a small, inconspicuous area for adhesion.
3. Use high-quality rollers and brushes designed for cabinet paints.
4. Allow for proper curing time—usually 7 to 14 days—before heavy use.
Avoid the mistake of rushing the drying process, and always ensure you have the right tools, like painting pyramids, to make the job easier and cleaner.
