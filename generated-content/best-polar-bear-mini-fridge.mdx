## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Polar Bear Mini Fridge

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Before making a purchase, you should decide exactly where the fridge will live. If it is for a bedroom, silence is the top priority. If it is for a vehicle, ensure the model includes a 12V DC adapter. Consider if you are mostly chilling beverages or if you need it for sensitive items like skincare and breast milk, which may require more stable thermal performance.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations ($35-$150 ranges)
Compact 4L models usually range from $35 to $60, making them very affordable gifts or personal accessories. Mid-sized 6L to 10L units often sit between $60 and $90. If you are looking for a large 25L premium collector model with digital controls, expect to pay anywhere from $120 to $150. Always factor in the cost of a replacement adapter if you plan to move the unit between home and car frequently.

### Key Features - Most important technical specifications
The most critical feature for these fridges is the cooling delta, which is how many degrees below the ambient room temperature it can reach. Most top models offer a 32-degree Fahrenheit reduction. Look for units with removable shelves if you plan to store taller cans or lunch boxes. Also, check if the unit has a warming function, as this adds significant versatility for food and baby bottles.

### Quality & Durability - What to look for in construction
