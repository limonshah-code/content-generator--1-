## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Product For Drain Flies

### 1. Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Before you purchase any treatment, you must identify where the flies are coming from. If they are in the kitchen, you need a food-safe, non-toxic formula that won't leave harmful residues near your prep areas. Always check if the product is safe for your specific plumbing type, especially if you have older metal pipes or a septic system.
