## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Queen Sleeper Sofa For Small Spaces

### 1. Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
Before you purchase, determine if the sofa will be your primary bed or just for occasional guests. If it is for daily use, prioritize models with high-density memory foam or spring coils like the Serta or Chic House models.
