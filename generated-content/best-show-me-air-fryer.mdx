## What to Look for Before Buying the Best Show Me Air Fryer

### Find Your Need - Compatibility and use case considerations
The first step is determining how you plan to use the device. If you are cooking for a family of four or more, a 5 or 6-quart basket is essential to avoid cooking in multiple batches. If you are a student or a single person, a 4-quart mini model will save you money and counter space. Consider if you want a basket-style for easy shaking or an oven-style for rotisserie and baking flexibility.

### Budget - Setting realistic price expectations ($50-$250 ranges)
